Community Rating And Empowerment Platform(CREP)

This project was inspired by the:

  • Insurgence of corruption and lack of service delivery in local government.
  • Limited avenues for citizens to:
    • Provide feedback
    • Hold elected officials accountable

The objective was to:

  • Develop a Citizen Rating App that allows residents to rate and review ward councilors.
  • Implement measures to ensure integrity and authenticity of user ratings.
  • Contribute to increased transparency and accountability in local government.

Home Page

The Home Page has:

  • Two carousels:
    • Best Rated Councilors
    • Worst Rated Councilors

    Here a user will discover the top-performing councilors with exceptional ratings of 4 and above, and gain insights into areas for improvement by exploring councilors with ratings below 3.

  • User Education Cards:

    Navigate through the app effortlessly with our user education cards, which provide clear instructions on how to create a free account, explore councilors from any ward nationwide, and contribute their ratings and feedback.

Signup Page

The Signup page features a comprehensive registration form consisting of 10 field. This form collects user personal information, which is essential for mapping them to their specific councilor which they will be able to rate.


  • Place/Area:

    • This field requires users' exact place of residence, ensuring accurate localization within their community

  • Email Address:

    • The user must ensure their valid email address, as a OneTimePin(OTP) will be sent to this email upon registration. They will use this unique pin to verify and activate their account

  • The dropdowns:

    • User must utilize these dropdown menus to select their Province, Municipality and Ward Number respectively. Pay special attention to the ward number dropdown, focusing on the last two digits to specify their precise location within their municipality.


    By providing these details accurately, they will gain access to a tailored experience within the app, enabling them to engage with their local councilor and contribute to the community-driven rating system effectively.

My Councilor
My Councilor
This feature has 3 dropdowns
  • Provinces:

    This is where a user selects the province they want to explore

  • Municipalities:

    This is where a user selects the municipality they want to explore. Selecting a municipality will yield a table of all the wards with respective councilors under that municipality. All the details including the average ratings are displayed.

  • Wards:

    Selecting a ward number yields the details of the councilor of that specific ward. This also comes with the rating form. On the rating form a user can select the service on which they wish to rate the councilor. The user will then select the number of stars they wish to give the councilor on the selected service. Submit will send the rating.
